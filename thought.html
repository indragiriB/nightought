* My opinion on doom scroll*

I think doom scroll is becoming a bad habit that we should avoid. Imagine waking up, glued to your phone. Phone is the first thing you grab after you wake up, how can this be more depressing? I think we as humans should take full control of ourselves. No matter how tempting our phone is, we have to say NO. Take a breath, get your minds right.
